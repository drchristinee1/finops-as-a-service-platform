# FinOps Self-Service Backlog

## Overview

This backlog defines prioritized automation and self-service capabilities that enable scalable FinOps operations.

It is managed as a product backlog, with continuous prioritization based on stakeholder impact, cost savings potential, and operational efficiency.

---

## Prioritization Principles

- Impact on cost reduction or avoidance
- Frequency of occurrence
- Stakeholder demand
- Ease of implementation
- Reduction in manual effort

---

## Backlog Items

### 1. Automated Anomaly Detection

**Description:**  
Detect unusual cost spikes across services and accounts.

**Outcome:**  
- Faster identification of cost issues  
- Reduced reliance on manual monitoring  

**Priority:** High

---

### 2. Cost Variance Notification System

**Description:**  
Trigger alerts when actual spend deviates from forecast or budget thresholds.

**Outcome:**  
- Early detection of budget overruns  
- Improved financial control  

**Priority:** High

---

### 3. Self-Service Cost Analysis Requests

**Description:**  
Allow stakeholders to submit structured requests for cost investigations.

**Outcome:**  
- Standardized intake process  
- Improved response time and consistency  

**Priority:** High

---

### 4. Rightsizing Recommendation Engine

**Description:**  
Identify underutilized resources and recommend appropriate sizing.

**Outcome:**  
- Reduced waste  
- Improved resource efficiency  

**Priority:** Medium

---

### 5. Commitment Optimization Dashboard

**Description:**  
Track Savings Plans and Reserved Instance coverage and utilization.

**Outcome:**  
- Improved effective cloud rates  
- Better commitment planning  

**Priority:** Medium

---

### 6. Tagging Compliance Automation

**Description:**  
Detect missing or incorrect tags and enforce tagging standards.

**Outcome:**  
- Improved cost allocation accuracy  
- Better accountability  

**Priority:** High

---

### 7. Idle Resource Detection

**Description:**  
Identify unused or underutilized resources such as unattached storage or idle compute.

**Outcome:**  
- Immediate cost reduction opportunities  
- Cleaner cloud environment  

**Priority:** High

---

### 8. Forecasting Support Tool

**Description:**  
Provide stakeholders with tools to model and forecast future cloud spend.

**Outcome:**  
- Improved budget planning  
- Better alignment with business growth  

**Priority:** Medium

---

## Backlog Management

The backlog is continuously refined through:

- Stakeholder feedback
- Analysis of recurring issues
- KPI performance (time-to-detect, time-to-act)
- Evolving business priorities

---

## Product Ownership Approach

This backlog is managed with a product mindset:

- Regular prioritization cycles
- Clear definition of outcomes
- Measurable success criteria
- Continuous delivery of incremental improvements
